Kedarkantha Trek (4 Night /5Days)

Pickup Service from Dehradun

Day 1: DEHRADUN - SANKRI

  • Distance: Approximately 190 km
  • Duration: Around 8–10 hours

Your adventure begins with a pickup from Prince Chowk, Dehradun, followed by a scenic drive to Sankri village via the charming hill station of Mussoorie. Upon arriving at Sankri, the Mountain Griffon team will warmly welcome you and introduce you to your fellow trekkers. This small, picturesque village is nestled amidst breathtaking mountain ranges and serves as the starting point for numerous trekking expeditions. After checking into your accommodation, take some time to explore the serene beauty and unique charm of Sankri. Enjoy evening tea and snacks, followed by a delicious dinner. Rest for the night in a cozy guest house or homestay, ensuring you're refreshed for the journey ahead.

Day 2: SANKRI - JAINOLA CAMP SITE

  • Distance: Approximately 8–10 km
  • Duration: Around 4–5 hours

Start your day with an early morning breakfast followed by a briefing session conducted by the trek leader, sharing all the necessary details about the trekking trails and safety instructions. The trek to the Janola campsite begins shortly after, taking you through serene forests, flowing streams, and rustic wooden bridges, offering a picturesque and adventurous experience. The trek covers a distance of approximately 8–10 km, and it will take about 4–5 hours to reach the campsite, depending on the pace and trail conditions. On arrival, enjoy a well-deserved lunch, followed by a relaxing acclimatization walk to adapt to the environment. End your day with evening snacks and dinner at the campsite, soaking in the tranquility of the surroundings.

Day 3: Juda Ka Talab - Kedarkantha Base Camp

  • Trek Distance: 3.5 km
  • Duration: 5 hours
  • Altitude: 11,020 ft

Today's trek takes you to two mesmerizing locations: Juda Ka Talab and the Kedarkantha Base Camp. The trail winds through dense oak and pine forests, offering a tranquil and scenic journey. Your first stop is the stunning frozen lake of Juda Ka Talab, where you’ll take a short break to soak in the serene beauty of the surroundings. After the break, the climb continues towards the Kedarkantha Base Camp, where you’ll be rewarded with breathtaking views of the surrounding peaks and a clear view of the Kedarkantha summit. Conclude your day with a hearty dinner and spend the night in tents under the starry sky, preparing for the next day’s adventure.

Day 4: Kedarkantha Base Camp -Summit and Return

  • Trek Distance: 9–10 km
  • Duration: 10–11 hours
  • Altitude: 12,500 ft

Today marks the most visually captivating day of the Kedarkantha trek. Begin with an early morning breakfast, and gear up for the climb to the Kedarkantha summit. The trail takes you through snow-covered slopes, narrow ridges, and stunning vistas. You'll rely on your trekking pole and crampons for a safe and steady ascent. Upon reaching the summit, you'll be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the majestic Himalayas, making every step worthwhile. After spending time at the summit, soaking in the scenery and capturing memories, begin your descent back to the Kedarkantha Base Camp. From there, continue the trek down to the Jainola campsite, where you’ll conclude the day with a hearty meal and a restful night in tents. You will have dinner and spend the night in tents.

Day 5: Jainola - Sankri | Departure

  • Trek Distance: 3 km
  • Duration (Trek): 3–4 hours
  • Drive Distance: 210 km

The trek concludes today, leaving you with countless happy memories to cherish. After an early breakfast, begin your descent from Jainola to Sankri. The trek takes approximately 3–4 hours, offering a final opportunity to soak in the beautiful surroundings. Upon arrival in Sankri, bid a heartfelt farewell to the Mountain Griffon team. After reminiscing on the journey and exchanging goodbyes, you will depart for your onward journey, taking home unforgettable experiences and stories.
