Auli Tour

Auli Tour Package (2 Nights, 3 Days) - Ex Rishikesh

Auli is a serene and beautiful hill station located in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand, India, at an altitude of approximately 2,800 meters. It is renowned for its breathtaking views of the snow-covered peaks of the Himalayan mountain range, including the iconic Nanda Devi, which is the second-highest peak in India. Auli is famous for its winter sports, especially skiing, making it one of the top skiing destinations in India. During the winter months, the slopes are covered with fresh snow, attracting adventure seekers and ski enthusiasts from all over the world.

Apart from skiing, Auli is also a haven for nature lovers and trekkers, offering stunning meadows, dense oak and deodar forests, and tranquil surroundings. The region is a great place to enjoy activities like trekking, camping, and nature walks, with several beautiful trails offering panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Auli is also well-connected by a cable car, which provides a unique aerial view of the landscape and is one of the longest cable car rides in Asia.

With its pristine beauty, pleasant weather, and a peaceful atmosphere, Auli has become a popular destination for both adventure enthusiasts and those looking to relax and reconnect with nature. Whether it's the thrill of skiing or simply soaking in the views, Auli offers a memorable experience for all.
