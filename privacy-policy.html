Privacy Policy

At MountainGriffon, we are committed to safeguarding your privacy. Below, we outline the principles governing the collection and use of your personal information when you visit our website.

  1. No Automatic Collection of Personal Information

    As a general rule, this website does not collect personal information about you when you visit the site. You can browse the site without revealing your identity unless you voluntarily choose to provide such information.

  2. Data Collection for Statistical Purposes

    This website logs your visits to enhance user experience and for statistical analysis. The following information may be recorded:

    • Your server's IP address.
    • The domain from which you access the internet (e.g., .com, .org, .in).
    • Your browser type.
    • Date and time of access.
    • Pages visited and files downloaded.
    • The previous internet address that linked you to this site.

    These logs do not identify users personally unless a law enforcement agency, under legal obligations, requests such information.

  3. Email Address Usage

    Your email address will only be recorded if you voluntarily send a message or subscribe to updates. It will be used solely for the intended purpose and not added to any mailing list or disclosed to third parties without your consent.

  4. Handling of Personal Information

    If you provide personal information for specific purposes, you will be informed of how it will be used. We ensure compliance with the highest data protection standards.

  5. Concerns and Feedback

    If you believe your privacy rights have been violated or have concerns about the handling of your information, please contact us via the Contact Us page.

  6. Definition of Personal Information

    The term “personal information” refers to any data that can identify an individual, either directly or indirectly.
