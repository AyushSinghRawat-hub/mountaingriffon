Kedarnath Yatra

Kedarnath is one of the holiest pilgrimage destinations in India, dedicated to Lord Shiva and part of both the Char Dham and Panch Kedar circuits. Located at an altitude of 11,755 feet in the Rudraprayag district of Uttarakhand, the temple is surrounded by towering snow-clad peaks, including the Kedarnath range, and sits near the source of the Mandakini River.
Believed to have been built by the Pandavas and later restored by Adi Shankaracharya, the ancient stone temple holds deep mythological and spiritual significance. The trek to Kedarnath is a 16-kilometer journey from Gaurikund, offering stunning views of lush valleys, cascading waterfalls, and rugged mountain terrain. For those unable to trek, helicopter services are also available. Kedarnath’s spiritual aura, combined with its breathtaking natural surroundings, makes it a must-visit for pilgrims and nature enthusiasts alike, offering a unique blend of devotion, adventure, and tranquility.

Kedarnath Yatra (3 Nights / 4 Days)

Day 1: Haridwar - Guptkashi

  • Distance covered: 204 km (8 hours)

Your journey begins with a morning pick-up from Haridwar, followed by a scenic drive to Guptkashi. Along the way, you will pass through the picturesque towns of Rishikesh, Byasi, Devprayag, and Srinagar, as well as the hill stations of Rudraprayag and Agastmuni. A stop for lunch will be made midway to refresh. Upon arrival in Guptkashi in the evening, check into your hotel and enjoy some leisure time. Dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 2: Guptkashi - Gaurikund and Kedarnath

  • Distance covered: 30 km (by road) + 16 km (trek)

Wake up early, refresh yourself, and prepare for an exciting day ahead. There are two ways to reach the Kedarnath temple, each offering a unique experience:

By Road: If you prefer traveling by road, your cab will take you to Sonprayag, which is 30 km away from Guptkashi. During the high season (May/June), this road can be quite busy, so it’s advisable to start early from Guptkashi. From Sonprayag, take local transport to Gaurikund, located 5 km ahead. Gaurikund serves as the base camp for the Kedarnath trek. After completing medical and biometric registration, begin the 16 km trek to Kedarnath, which typically takes around 5 hours. For those who wish to skip the trek, ponies or a doli are available.

By Air: Alternatively, you can take a helicopter ride for a faster and more scenic journey. The helicopter ride offers a breathtaking bird's-eye view of the snow-clad Himalayan peaks. Your cab will take you to the helipad, where you'll complete the necessary formalities before flying to Kedarnath.

Upon arrival in Kedarnath, check into your hotel, rest, and explore the beautiful surroundings at your leisure. Enjoy a peaceful dinner and overnight stay at the hotel in Kedarnath.

Day 3: Kedarnath - Sonprayag - Guptkashi

  • Distance covered: 16 km (trek) + 30 km (by road)

Wake up early, get refreshed, and prepare for a spiritual morning. Begin your day with shopping for pooja items at Kedarnath temple. You can also opt for a special pooja ceremony at additional charges. After offering your prayers and completing the pooja at the Kedarnath temple, head back to your hotel to pack and check out.

After breakfast, begin your descent back from Kedarnath. You can either trek down or take a helicopter ride back to Sonprayag. Once in Sonprayag, board your cab and proceed to Guptkashi, located approximately 30 km away. The drive will take about an hour. Enjoy lunch at a midway stop.

Arrive at Guptkashi by evening, check into your hotel, and relax. Spend some time exploring the area, witnessing the beautiful sunset, and enjoying a bonfire with light refreshments. Dinner and an overnight stay at the hotel in Guptkashi.

Day 4: Guptkashi - Haridwar

  • Distance covered: 201 km (Approx. 6 hours)

Start your day with a hearty breakfast at the hotel. After checking out, begin your journey to Haridwar. En route, take some time to explore the vibrant local market of Rishikesh. Visit iconic landmarks like Ram Jhula and Laxman Jhula. Enjoy a delicious lunch at a local restaurant in Rishikesh's market.

Post-lunch, continue your drive towards Haridwar. Upon arrival, you'll be dropped off at your designated location, marking the conclusion of your spiritual journey.
